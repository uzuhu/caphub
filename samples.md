# Custom Orders & Sample Process

Because every application is unique, we help you define electrical and mechanical parameters to create the perfect capacitor.

## Customization workflow

1. **Send inquiry** with desired specs
2. **Our engineers propose** a design-in alternative
3. **Sample agreement** – we produce 5–50 pcs for validation
4. **Testing & feedback** – you perform electrical/thermal tests
5. **Mass production** after approval

## Sample policy

- Sample unit price: same as mass production price
- **Shipping cost**: paid by customer (FedEx/DHL/UPS)
- Samples are fully representative of final product quality
- For extensive custom mechanical changes, small tooling fee may apply

## What we need from you

- Target operating voltage & capacitance tolerance
- Ripple current RMS value & frequency
- Expected life hours & ambient temperature
- Mounting type: snap-in / screw terminal
- Max dimensions (diameter x length)

> **Lead times:** Samples: 7-14 days. Mass production: 3-5 weeks.
